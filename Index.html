The new roadster was designed by former Mazda designer Franz von Holzhausen. It has a removable glass roof and a body with two front seats plus two small seats in the back.

The roadster has three electric motors, one at the front and two at the rear. They provide four-wheel drive as well as an addressable torque distribution system for better cornering.
